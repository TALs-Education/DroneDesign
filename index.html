Drone Design Lab

An educational drone design environment where students compare component choices, measure thrust and current in the lab, and learn stabilization on a constrained drone rig.

Open Drone Designer View Selected Design Ver 1

Lab Learning Path

The project lets students experiment with optional drone designs before moving into measurement and control work. Students collect thrust versus current data for different motor and propeller combinations, then use those measurements to reason about battery, driver, propeller, and flight-controller choices.

1. Explore designs

Use the designer to compare batteries, motors, propellers, ESCs, payload, and optional add-ons.

2. Measure propulsion

In the lab, measure thrust versus current for multiple motors and propellers.

3. Stabilize a fixed drone

Work with a drone attached to a load cell for lift measurement and constrained in all axes except one rotational axis.

4. Full control practice

Practice control in a protected environment.
